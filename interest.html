Interest in IT

IT has been an obsession of mine as far back as I can remember, I was fortunate enough to get my first PC at 8 years old when the company my mum worked for were upgrading their systems. While the majority of my time on computers was playing video games with a bit of rudimentary photo editing, but on February 6th, 2018, when SpaceX's Falcon Heavy test flight happened and I watched those side boosters landing side by side as if straight out of science fiction the passion and excitement for the ever- evolving world of technology was ignited like never before.

With such a strong passion for IT choosing to study at RMIT was really a no brainer. With an excellent global reputation and courses run by experts in their field, who are ensuring that the information and skills they teach are relevant in a constantly evolving industry. Having not studied for many years I was a little nervous to take the plunge, but right out of the gate the incredible staff and their knowledge/support has made me wish I did this years ago.

The goal I have for my studies at RMIT is to gather as much knowledge and relevant skills as I can across a range of areas within the IT industry. While I have dream companies I would love to work for, the exact pathway and the position at those companies isn't so clear. My intension is to get hands on with a variety of disciplines throughout the industry to find the area that I think I have the potential to excel at.
